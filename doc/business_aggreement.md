# **zetuTech Seller Agreement**

**Effective Date:** Upon Seller Registration
**Parties:**

* **ZetuTech Ltd** (“Platform”)
* **You, the registered business** (“Seller”)

---

## **1. Definition of Terms**

* **Platform** – ZetuTech online marketplace.
* **Seller** – a business approved to list products.
* **Buyer** – a customer purchasing through the platform.
* **Commission** – 12% fee deducted per successful order.
* **Products** – electronics offered by the Seller.

---

## **2. Purpose of Agreement**

This Agreement outlines the terms under which the Seller may list and sell electronics products on ZetuTech.

---

## **3. Seller Eligibility**

The seller must:

1. Submit accurate business details
2. Provide valid contact information
3. Sell only electronics and related appliances
4. Agree to product authenticity requirements
5. Comply with Tanzanian laws and consumer protection standards

ZetuTech reserves the right to approve or reject applications.

---

## **4. Product Listing Rules**

Sellers must ensure:

* All product information is accurate
* Prices are correct
* Product images reflect actual items
* Warranty details (if any) are clearly stated
* Stock levels are updated

Fake or misleading product information is prohibited.

---

## **5. Commission & Fees**

* ZetuTech charges **12% commission per successful order**.
* No subscription fees
* No listing fees
* Commission is automatically deducted before Seller payout.

---

## **6. Payments & Settlements**

* Buyers pay through **M-Pesa, TigoPesa, or Airtel Money**.
* Payments are held until order completion.
* Seller receives payouts to their registered merchant number.

---

## **7. Order Fulfillment Responsibilities**

Sellers must:

* Process orders on time
* Provide accurate delivery information
* Package products securely
* Respond to customer inquiries
* Honor warranties or return policies they provide

Failure to fulfill orders may result in penalties or suspension.

---

## **8. Returns, Refunds & Disputes**

ZetuTech may:

* Mediate disputes
* Approve refunds if product is defective, misrepresented, or undelivered

Sellers must cooperate with dispute resolution.

---

## **9. Prohibited Activities**

Sellers may NOT:

* Sell counterfeit products
* Engage in fraud or price manipulation
* Bypass the platform to avoid commission
* Use abusive or misleading language with customers

Violations may result in account termination.

---

## **10. Intellectual Property**

Sellers must only upload images or descriptions they own or have permission to use.

---

## **11. Data & Privacy**

Seller data is protected and used only for:

* Platform operations
* Legal compliance
* Fraud prevention

ZetuTech does not sell or misuse seller data.

---

## **12. Termination of Agreement**

ZetuTech may suspend or terminate a seller account for:

* Policy violations
* Fraud
* Repeated customer complaints
* Illegal activity

Either party may terminate the agreement with notice.

---

## **13. Limitation of Liability**

ZetuTech is not responsible for:

* Product quality
* Delivery delays
* Seller’s financial losses
* External payment failures

The seller holds full responsibility for their products and business operations.

---

## **14. Modifications to Agreement**

ZetuTech may update the agreement.
Sellers will be notified of significant changes.

---

## **15. Acceptance**

By registering as a Seller, you acknowledge that you have **read, understood, and agreed** to this Seller Agreement.
