# **ZetuTech – Business Plan (Electronics-Only Marketplace)**

### *A Tanzania-focused online marketplace for buying and selling electronics*

---

# **1. Executive Summary**

ZetuTech is a specialized e-commerce marketplace for electronics in Tanzania, allowing verified sellers to list and sell products such as TVs, laptops, desktops, cameras, sound systems, smart watches, phones, gaming equipment, and home appliances.
The platform operates under a **commission-based revenue model** (12% per sale), with secure mobile-money payments (M-Pesa, TigoPesa, Airtel Money), buyer protection, and a robust seller verification process to eliminate counterfeits and scams.

ZetuTech aims to become **the #1 trusted electronics marketplace in Tanzania**, solving the biggest problems in local electronics buying: lack of trust, fake products, low transparency, and scattered supply.

---

# **2. Problem Statement**

### Current challenges in Tanzania’s electronics market:

* **High prevalence of fake electronics and stolen goods**
* Lack of **trust** between buyers and sellers
* **Fragmented market** (Instagram shops, WhatsApp groups, street shops)
* No strong electronics-focused online marketplace
* **No buyer protection** or dispute resolution system
* Weak product verification and no standardized pricing
* Poor customer experience: no organized reviews, specs, or comparisons

These challenges create **risk**, **confusion**, and **lost money** for both buyers and sellers.

---

# **3. ZetuTech Solution**

ZetuTech provides:

### ✔ A specialized marketplace ONLY for electronics

Focused categories allow deep product specs, filtering, reviews, and trust-building.

### ✔ Verified sellers only

Sellers must submit a **Business Registration Request** → reviewed → approved.

### ✔ Secure mobile-money payments

Integrated with **M-Pesa**, **TigoPesa**, **Airtel Money**.

### ✔ Buyer protection

Funds are held until the buyer confirms they have received the product.

### ✔ High-quality product listings

Structured attributes for each electronics category: RAM, CPU, screen size, storage, warranty, condition, etc.

### ✔ Transparent pricing

Market-driven pricing, recommendations, and comparison tools.

### ✔ Multilingual (Swahili + English)

Serving all Tanzanians effectively.

# **6. Revenue Model (Core = 12% Commission)**

### **Monetization Streams**

1. **12% Commission per successful sale**

   * Deducted automatically during payout
   * Example:

     * Product price: 500,000 TZS
     * Commission (12%): 60,000 TZS
     * Payout to seller: 440,000 TZS

2. **Future revenue streams** (phase 2–3):

   * Featured listings (2,000–10,000 TZS per boost)
   * Ads for brands (TVs, smartphones, accessories)
   * Fulfillment service (logistics fee)
   * Extended warranties
   * Data insights for vendors
   * Subscription for “ZetuTech Premium Seller”
